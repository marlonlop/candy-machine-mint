Hellish Party Boolloons


The Hellish Party is the one and only fair in the netherworld. The Boolloons are the first collection based on this hellish fair and they will consist of 4,666 Boolloon NFTs and 100 art concept pre-release Boolloon NFTs. Each Boolloon is an evil, grim, and unique character, no two are the same; some are rarer than others. There are several traits that makes them unique.

These hellish Boolloons are created from hand made pieces of clay and they will grant you access to our upcoming Hellish Party fair.
